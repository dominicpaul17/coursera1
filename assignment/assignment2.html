BEST NUTS IN THE WORLD

ALMONDS

Asian parents often give their children almonds on exam days. This is with good reason: Compounds found in almonds help regulate the production of an important hormone in the brain called dopamine. Dopamine is a key player in proper brain development, sharpens the memory, and boosts mood. Almonds are one of the best nuts for health as well. Eat these soaked nuts in the early part of your day.

BRAZILNUTS

Brazil nuts are easy to spot, as they are among the largest in size. They are actually seeds, not nuts. As their name suggests, Brazil nuts come from a tree originating in South America.Brazil nuts are very rich in selenium, a compound which allows the body to digest protein. Selenium is an important anti-cancer nutrient and is linked to good prostrate health.

CASHEWS

Many will testify that cashew nuts are among of the most delicious nuts in the planet. They taste yummy when eaten alone, but are also used to top off many dishes and desserts around the world.Cashew nuts get bad press because of their high fat content. However, they are actually high in monounsaturated fatty acids, which are valuable for the heart.
